Abstract - College Admission Predictor system is a web-based application system in which students can register their marks along with their personal information. This helps to predict their admission in colleges. Administrator can add the college details and the batch details. Using this application, the allotment of seats is efficient and easier.

Administrator has the power of allotment. Admin can add the allotted seats into the file and the details are saved into the system. This helps students make the right decisions for choosing the college.
